About Us

An establishment born in 1900 from a family with a passion for high-quality cuisine and ingredients, focusing on local culinary traditions. Originally a modest grocery store, it has evolved while maintaining its unique identity, offering products with a modern twist and a commitment to quality, professionalism, and personalized service. Through new methodologies, it strives to enhance efficiency and modernize processes while preserving its familiar atmosphere and selecting products that honor local traditions and the 'Made in Italy' label. Vino & Amore is a gourmet deli that promises a warm and welcoming atmosphere, thanks to its friendly and professional staff.
